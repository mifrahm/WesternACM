About Us

The Western Association of Computing and Mathematics (WACM) is a newly established university club aimed to represent the computing, mathematics and data science students at Western Sydney University.

Our vision is to bring together and empower the students of the to-be established School of Computing, Data and Mathematics at WSU.

WACM aims to organise events to enhance both the student experience and build networking opportunities for our computing and mathematics students. We also aim to bridge the gap between students and employers by promoting professional development and fostering creative thinking to build a culture of entrepreneurship within its members.
